Our Undertale Project

By: Ella Galioto, Jack Norton, and Kelly Anderson.

For our project we are analyzing text through major characters significant to the multiple routes in the game. We have assigned a route to each person; Kelly is doing the Pacifist route, Jack is doing the Neutral route, and Ella is doing the Genocide route.

What exactly is Undertale?

Undertale is a story-driven RPG game created by Toby Fox in 2015. In the game, you play as a child who falls into the Underground full of monsters. The choices you make in the game influence your outcomes, you can fight enemies or spare them and make friends instead. The game is known for its humor, gameplay, emotional storytelling, and multiple endings based on how you treat others.
